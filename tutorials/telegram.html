Using Telegram Bot

In this tutorial you will learn how to use a simpler version of our AI trading bot - instead of using DAPP with MetaMask or Trust wallet, you can also use it with simple Telegram bot which is also called BscAiBot.

When you open BscAiBot on Telegram and click start, you will see a welcome message, main menu at the bottom and secondary menu in the bottom left corner. Bellow welcome message, you will see an airdrop button that you can use to claim your airdrop. These are the options of the main menu:

  • Stats
  • Mint BSCAI
  • Invite
  • Help

Stats button provides you with your bot statistics like how many BSCAI you have, its current price, how long is it staked for, your reward stats and referral stats. It also provides you with the menu for actions regarding your staked BSCAI and rewards.

Use compound to add your reward to the existing stake. Do this daily for optimized yield and highest APY. Withdraw button allows you to withdraw your rewards directly into BNB to your withdrawal address, both staking and referral rewards. You can use unstake option only after your staking period has expired and then you can withdraw your staked BSCAI directly into BNB. Airdrop button is here if you missed it in the welcome message.

Mint BSCAI button allows you to mint new BSCAI that will be staked for 3, 6 or 12 months. After you click mint button, it will provide you with BNB deposit address. Send BNB to that address, click the button bellow after you send BNB and you will get a menu where you can choose for how long you want to stake your newly minted BSCAI. If you choose 3 monts, your APY will be 887%, 6 months for 1275% and 12 months for 1833%.

Invite button gives you your invite link together with an optional message that you can use to invite other people. When you invite people, you get 10% for their actions, both for claiming airdrop and when they mint new BSCAI. In case of minting, you get 10% of BNB directly to your account and your can withdraw it right away.

Help button provides basic help with the bot and its description.

There is also a secondary menu in the bottom left corner with two commands: one for setting your BSC (BNB) address for withdrawal and the other for setting your Twitter / X username. To use them, tap on menu, then tap on one of the commands, hold it, wait for it to be written in the chat window and then enter you address or Twitter username.
