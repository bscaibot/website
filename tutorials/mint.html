Mint BSCAI Token

In this tutorial you will learn how to mint BSCAI token using BNB on Binance Smart Chain with one of three staking options (3, 6 and 12 months time lock).

To mint BSCAI token, you need to have a certain amount of BNB (or WBNB to be precise since it's on Binance Smart Chain - BSC). Open our DAPP in your Trust or MetaMask wallet and connect it.

Scroll down to "BSCAI Toolbox" section and "Mint BSCAI Token" subsection. Enter amount of BNB you want to use, optionally calculate the amount of BSCAI you will receive and then click "Mint" button. Your newly minted BSCAI will be staked and you can choose staking time on which your staking reward depends:

  • 3 months staking brings you daily reward of 0.1% BSCAI
  • 6 months staking brings you daily reward of 0.2% BSCAI
  • 12 months staking brings you daily reward of 0.3% BSCAI

Keep in mind that staking rewards is not your only way of earning, you also earn 0.5% daily by token price increase.
