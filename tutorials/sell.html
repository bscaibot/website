Sell BSCAI Token

This tutorial will teach you how to sell your BSCAI tokens for profit using either our DAPP or PancakeSwap DEX.

First you have to differentiate staked BSCAI and BSCAI in your wallet. BSCAI that's placed in your wallet can only be sold using PancakeSwap DEX. You can find a tutorial on how to sell tokens using PancakeSwap here.

You can also sell your reward that you receive in BSCAI using PancakeSwap, but it's much smarter to compound it, increase your reward and then sell all after your staking period has expired or to re-stake it for more profit.

To sell your staked BSCAI token, you need to wait for staking period to expire (it can be 3, 6 or 12 months and your reward depends on it). After the staking period expires, you have two options, to withdraw and sell automatically using BscAiBot smart contract (in our DAPP) or to simply withdraw and sell using PancakeSwap and your wallet.

Please notice that by compounding your reward daily, you get a much better effect with compound interest "magic".
