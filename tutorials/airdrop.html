Claim BSCAI Airdrop

In this tutorial you will learn how to claim BSCAI airdrop using MetaMask or Trust wallet and our DAPP. You need to have small amount of BNB for gas.

To claim BSCAI token through airdrop, open our DAPP in your Trust or MetaMask wallet and connect it.

DAPP URL:
dapp.bscaibot.com

DAPP URL copied...

Open menu and click the airdrop option. After that, follow instructions to claim the airdrop. You will need these to successfully claim it:

  • MetaMask or Trust wallet
  • A small amount of BNB for gas
  • Telegram account
  • Twitter account

After you claim the airdrop, you will receive 1 staked BSCAI. You can see the price of BSCAI here.
