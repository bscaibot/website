  How is your APY (Annual Percentage Yield) so high?

We are still in very early stages of beta testing so our bot is using fairly small amount of capital and because of that achieving insanely high APY. This APY will most certainly fall as bot's base capital grows, but it's very hard to predict how much it will fall. We will be transparent about it.

  Do you have a company?

We are located in Republic of Korea (South Korea) and we had trouble with regulation when we tried to incorporate a company here so now we're researching regulation in Singapore and some other jurisdictions. As soon as we incorporate the company, we will publish all the information here on the website.

  How is BscAiBot decentralized?

Not all parts of BscAiBot are decentralized and for some parts it will probably never be possible. The bot that does trading is a centralized service that we manage and because of its nature, it will probably be impossible to decentralize it. Our token however is published on BSC chain and is decentralized and it has a decentralized BSC token called BSCAI and decentralized smart contract (DAPP) for managing bot's capital, BSCAI tokens in circulation, selling and buying token etc. Telegram bot is by its nature also a centralized service which works as a simpler version of the DAPP. DAPP is intended for more advanced users and Telegram bot for regular users.
