Introduction

BSCAI is a BSC-based ERC-20 token, representing co-ownership in a BSC network AI trading bot, with a 1M max supply and staking feature.

BSCAI is a new cryptocurrency based on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) network, utilizing the ERC-20 standard. With a maximum supply of 1 million tokens, BSCAI represents a unique and innovative solution for co-ownership in BscAiBot, an artificial intelligence trading bot for the BSC network and its decentralized exchanges (DEXes). The BSCAI project aims to bring together the power of decentralized finance (DeFi) and artificial intelligence (AI) to create a self-sustaining and profitable ecosystem for its users.

Background

BSCAI brings together AI and DeFi on the Binance Smart Chain network, offering a seamless and efficient trading experience through its AI trading bot, BscAiBot, on multiple DEXes.

The world of decentralized finance (DeFi) has seen rapid growth and innovation in recent years, with new projects and solutions emerging regularly. However, one area that has seen limited development is the use of artificial intelligence (AI) in DeFi. BSCAI aims to bridge this gap, bringing together the power of AI and DeFi to create a unique and innovative solution for co-ownership in a BSC network AI trading bot.

Token Use Case

BSCAI is a currency used for co-ownership, profit sharing, and voting on decisions in BscAiBot.

BSCAI is the currency used for co-ownership and decision-making in BscAiBot. Each BSCAI token represents a share of ownership in the trading bot, and holders are entitled to a portion of the profits generated by the bot. Additionally, BSCAI token holders will have the ability to vote on various aspects of the bot's operation, such as risk management and asset allocation.

Token Distribution

1 million BSCAI tokens will be distributed through a fair process, with no pre-sale or private sale, following a specified distribution plan.

The initial supply of BSCAI tokens will be 1 million, distributed through a fair and transparent process. No pre-sale or private sale will be held. The tokens will be distributed as follows:

  • 50% to the community through a public sale
  • 20% to the development team
  • 20% to a community treasury for future development and maintenance
  • 10% to advisors and early backers

Staking Feature

BSCAI offers a staking feature with 3 time-lock options, earning higher rewards for longer lock-up periods, providing passive income and network security.

BSCAI offers a staking feature with three staking options in terms of time locking: 3 months, 6 months, and 12 months. Staking BSCAI tokens allows users to earn passive income while contributing to the security and stability of the network. The longer the lock-up period, the higher the rewards.

Technology

BSCAI operates on Binance Smart Chain, utilizing ERC-20 standard, and BscAiBot combines machine learning and smart contracts for efficient trading across multiple DEXes.

BSCAI is built on the Binance Smart Chain network, utilizing the ERC-20 standard for compatibility with existing wallets and exchanges. The AI trading bot, BscAiBot, is built using a combination of machine learning algorithms and smart contracts. The bot will be able to execute trades on multiple DEXes simultaneously, maximizing profitability while minimizing risk.

Security

BSCAI prioritizes security, implementing regular audits, bug bounties, multi-signature wallets, and transparent smart contract transactions.

Security is a top priority for BSCAI. The team has implemented multiple security measures, including regular audits, bug bounties, and multi-signature wallets. Additionally, the use of smart contracts ensures that all transactions are transparent and verifiable.
